5.4 Technical AEO Requirements

Robots.txt Configuration

Content Structure for AI Citation

  • Lead with answers: Put the direct answer in your first paragraph
  • Use definitive statements: "The best approach is..." not "might be..."
  • Include specific data: Numbers, percentages, dates
  • Structure with H2 → H3 → bullets: 40% more likely to be cited
  • Add author credentials: Named experts with qualifications
  • Update regularly: Fresh content gets priority in AI responses
